Executive Summary

The BIM3D Hub is a groundbreaking technology designed to address one of the most profound and costly challenges in the Architecture, Engineering, Construction (AEC) and Industrial Design industries:



The enormous waste of resources spent manually fixing incompatible 3D object formats.



BIM3D Hub ensures that a single block (complete with geometry, materials, and BIM metadata) functions consistently across any software, without rework or loss of consistency.

The Problem

A Fragmented Industry

For years, productivity has been hampered by the absence of an operational standard. This leads to information loss, high modeling costs, and constant rework to generate multiple versions of the same object.

The Solution

Universal Format

The .b3do format carries geometry, PBR materials, and BIM metadata in a single, intelligent container.

Multi-Platform

Native plugins for SketchUp, Blender, Rhino, Revit, and ArchiCAD coming soon.
