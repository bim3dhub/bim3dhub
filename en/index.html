Executive Summary

The BIM3D Hub is a groundbreaking technology designed to address one of the most profound and costly challenges in the Architecture, Engineering, Construction (AEC) and Industrial Design industries:

The enormous waste of resources spent manually fixing incompatible 3D object formats.

BIM3D Hub ensures that a single block (complete with geometry, materials, and BIM metadata) functions consistently across any software, without rework or loss of consistency.

The Problem

The AEC industry operates in a fragmented environment
with dozens of incompatible formats

Critical Consequences

  • Continuous rework to generate multiple versions of the same object
  • Loss of information during software conversions
  • High costs for modelling and library maintenance
  • Adoption limited by structural incompatibilities

The Solution

Universal .b3do Format

An intelligent 3D container that combines:

  • Optimized Geometry: Clean, efficient mesh data
  • PBR Materials: Physically-based rendering materials
  • IFC Metadata: Industry Foundation Classes integration
  • Parametric Properties: Manufacturer-specific attributes
